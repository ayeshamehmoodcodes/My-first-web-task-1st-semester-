SHERLOCK HOLMES

Sherlock is a British crime television series based on Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's
Sherlock Holmes detective stories.

       

In this updated take on Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's beloved mystery tales, the eccentric sleuth
prowls the streets of modern London in search of clues.
