SEASON 3

After two years' absence, "deceased" Sherlock Holmes emerges from hiding and asks Watson to help him solve three perplexing and dangerous cases.

EPISODE 1: THE EMPTY HEARSE

Two years after Sherlock's "death," Watson has moved on. But when
London is threatened by a terrorist attack, Sherlock decides it's time to
return.

                

EPISODE 2: THE SIGN OF THREE

While mortal danger stalks Watson's wedding reception, Sherlock faces his
biggest challenge of all: delivering a best man's speech!

                

EPISODE 3: HIS LAST VOW

A case of stolen letters leads Sherlock Holmes to engage in a long conflict
with the powerful Charles Augustus Magnussen, the Napoleon of blackmail.

                
EPISODE 4: THE ABOMINABLE BRIDE

In this special set in 1895, Holmes and Watson investigate a vengeful
apparition in a wedding dress, reportedly the ghost of a suicide victim.
